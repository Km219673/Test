The Second Missionary

The second missionary journey started from 49-51 AD. He started his journey with Silas. He revisited the cities where he traveled for his first journey. Then he wanted to go to Asian Province but he was stopped by the Holy Spirit which made them follow the journey toward Troas. He had a vision about a man who as inviting Paul for an immediate visit. After, Troas, they went to Phillipi where they met Lydia. Paul was put to prison as per the order by the Lydia because Paul healed Lydia's slave and the slave was baptized. From there, Paul and Silas arrived to the Thessalonica then Athen. Paul actively preached the teaching of Jesus despite many difficulties. He persuades his journey to the Ephesus to finally to Antioch.
